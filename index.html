Last Prototypes

PLANCH

Planch is a remake of Carcassonne game by Klaus-Jürgen Wrede. The game is composed of 84 different cards that players will put in turns to build the board game. They can build roads, cities, fields and unusual places. Players earn points based on the size of their buildings.

GRAPHIC DICESIONS

The "dicesions" are a series of wooden dice for creating a random layout. The graphic designers delegate the decision to the dice without spending long hours thinking about its composition. The creative process become a playful moment and the uncontrolled result is unexpected.

ICE CREAM CARDS

Ice Cream Cards is a game of 54 traditional cards of different types of ice cream and popsicle. This deck of playing cards is designed to support the ice creams who die every summer because of the heat.
